Programmable Photonic Circuits

The paper Programmable Photonic Circuits [1] sparked my interest, as it was never something I considered possible. My only knowledge of photonic circuits prior to reading this was for use in networking, with devices such as trancievers and photonic switches. The idea that light can be used in the context of computing especially drew my interest. And so I would like to share what I have learned about these devices from this paper, and discuss the potential applications that I find particularly interesting.

Alexander Montgomerie-Corcoran, 3rd September 2021

[1] Wim Bogaerts, Daniel Pérez, José Capmany, David A. B. Miller, Joyce Poon, Dirk Englund, Francesco Morichetti & Andrea Melloni. Programmable Photonic Circuits, Oct 2020.
